About the Seminar

This seminar explores how AI accelerates research, enables breakthroughs, and shapes the future of innovation across fields. Join us to learn about real-world applications, opportunities, and challenges at the intersection of AI and science.

Join Discord (for Zoom link) | Watch on YouTube | Follow on X | Join the Mailing List

If you are on the UChicago campus, welcome to join us in person in Conference Room 322 at the Data Science Institute (5460 S University Ave).
